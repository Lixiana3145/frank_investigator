## 📌 What this app does

frank_investigator helps you review news stories and look for signs of bias, weak sources, and claims that need a closer look. It gives you a simple way to check articles before you trust or share them.

Use it to:
- scan a news article for review
- compare claims against source signals
- spot wording that may show bias
- keep notes on articles you want to revisit
- sort stories by how trustworthy they look

## 🔍 How to use it

### 1. Open an article
Paste the article text or load a saved item from your computer. The app is built to handle plain news text, links, and copied article content.

### 2. Review key claims
Look at the main claims one by one. Focus on:
- names
- dates
- numbers
- quotes
- statements that sound one-sided

### 3. Check for bias signals
The app helps you look for common signs of bias, such as:
- strong emotional language
- missing context
- loaded words
- one-sided framing
- claims without clear support

### 4. Compare source quality
Use the built-in review tools to judge whether the article depends on:
- named sources
- unnamed sources
- repeated claims from the same outlet
- weak or circular references

### 5. Save your notes
Store your notes so you can compare multiple articles later. This is useful when you want to track the same story across different outlets.
